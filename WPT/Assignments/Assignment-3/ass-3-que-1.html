It is rightly said that being disciplined is essential in life.When a persons leads a disciplined life, they set an easy path to success. they will develop an approach to happiness and a beautiful future ahead . Being disciplined is the practice of having a scheduled daily routine which helps an individual to be punctual and hard-working. An individual is taught what being disciplined means from the time they are young. Even though discipline takes a lot of effort , the advantages of a disciplined life make it a worthwhile endeavour.
